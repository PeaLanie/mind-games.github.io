Beginner Mode

Congratulations for making it to beginner mode!...were you expand your addition skills to the next level. Instead of adding intergers between 1 and 9 now you have to consider a zero after each digit. Your growth in this mode is dependant on the previous mode (Basic Addition)

The method is the same but with the consideration of zero. Remember this is a lifetime skill that you can use anytime in your life

The Theory:

(Think you already now how to add natural numbers? Jump straight to the game)

In this mode you pretend as if the zero is invisible and add numbers like you did in the Basic Addition Mode. After adding the numbers you insert the zero in the end. Look at some exampes below:

How to add up a list of real numbers between 10 and 90 with increments of 10?

Example #1

To understand these examples you must be familiar with the Basics of Addition. Follow the steps below to understand the method behind adding real numbers between 10 and 90 with increments of 10.

  1. Hide zero from the mix.

  2. Add the numbers like you would with basic intergers between 1 and 9. If you're not familiar with this method I would strongly recommend that you go back to the Basic Addition Mode.

  3. Remember the zero we hid on step one? Now is the time to insert it back. Remember that because your list is all in the 10's form; so you only insert one zero back.

  4. Now that is your final answer: 170
